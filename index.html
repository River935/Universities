World Universities


Welcome to the searchable database of Universities around the world!
On our website, you can find universities all over the world with links to
the web pages of them. You can also search for universities in different countries
and find a university even if you know only part of its name. You can view all universities
in any country and find their number. You can also check out the interactive map and explore
how many universities are in each country. You can also create your own list of favourite universities.
And in the end, when you have decided on the universities you like, you can add all the necessary
application deadlines and other information to your personal calendar.
Enjoy and find your dream university!
